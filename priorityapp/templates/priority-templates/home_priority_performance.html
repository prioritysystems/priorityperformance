About Priority Performance Projects

  • 2020

    Priority Performance Projects (PPP) was founded in 2020 by Dr Sifiso Falala as a multi-layered project facilitation and execution company. The aim of the business is to advance development, economic change and growth in the developing world.

  • Projects

    Priority Performance Projects (PPP) identified the disconnect between the developing world's resources and optimisation of resource exploitation for the benefit of those in the third world. It seeks to close this gap by identifying and championing projects that are instrumental in this regard.

  • Performance

    PPP is selective about what it considers to be priority projects. At the centre of the companyâ€™s ethos is performance, which is a result of on time problem identification and competent delivery. The company undertakes both public and private priority projects. The unique skills of PPP are in stakeholder engagement, process design, financing, implementation and monitoring. The main benefit of working with us are objective attainment, stakeholder satisfaction, time and cost saving.

Priority Performance Team

Dr Sifiso Falala

Dr Sifiso Falala is the CEO and founder of Plus 94 Research, a company that he co-founded in 1998. He is a trained Demographer with a Master of Science in Population Studies. He holds a PhD in Management of Technology and Innovation from the Da Vinci Institute.

Dr Pulane Molokwane

Dr Pulane Elsie Molokwane is a Nuclear Physicist and an Environmental Specialist with expertise in ground water remediation, nuclear waste, process and solid waste management. She is a registered Radiation Physicist and holds a PhD in Environmental Engineering, M.Sc. (Applied Radiation Science and Technology), Post-Graduate Diploma (Applied Radiation Sciences and Technology) and B.Sc.(Physics).

Jason Mudau

Jason Mudau is a Research and Development Director at Plus 94 Research. He specialises in technological research and systems development. He holds a B.Sc. Degree in Applied Mathematics and Statistics.
